# @prefix Declaration

*Concept*

Declares a short alias for a namespace IRI. Example: @prefix rdf: &lt;http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#&gt; . Prefixes make Turtle readable -- without them every IRI needs full angle-bracket notation.

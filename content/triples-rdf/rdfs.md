# RDF Schema (RDFS)

*Concept*

A vocabulary for describing classes and properties: rdfs:Class, rdfs:subClassOf, rdfs:domain, rdfs:range, rdfs:label, rdfs:comment. Reckons.AI uses rdfs:label for human-readable entity names.

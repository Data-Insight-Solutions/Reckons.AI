# Data Insight Solutions — Brand Brief

## Company

Data Insight Solutions LLC is a data consultancy that helps businesses collect, automate, integrate, and display data to drive informed decisions and sustainable growth.

## Tagline

"Transform data into actionable insights."

## Core Services

### 1. Collect
Establishing reliable data sources through web forms, APIs, databases, and communication platforms. Technologies: PowerApps, Fillable PDFs, VOIP, Zoom, custom forms.

### 2. Automate
Building workflows for data transformation, validation, and business logic. Uses both low-code and full-code solutions, including approval flows, scheduled triggers, and event-driven pipelines.

### 3. Integrate
Connecting disparate systems via APIs, REST, SOAP, SDKs, and ELT processes to create unified data sources. Covers CRM, ERP, marketing platforms, accounting, and legacy systems.

### 4. Display
Creating dashboards, reports, and visualizations. Tools: Power BI, custom web dashboards, in-app reporting, PDF exports. Focus on real-time monitoring and executive summaries.

## Brand Identity

- **Tone:** Professional, solution-oriented, technically credible, approachable
- **Primary Color:** Deep navy (#1a2332)
- **Accent Color:** Teal (#1a9b8e)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Logo:** Text-based "Data Insight Solutions" with a subtle data/graph motif
- **Photography Style:** Tech-forward, collaboration-focused, conceptual data imagery

## Target Audience

- Small to mid-sized businesses (50–500 employees)
- Operations managers, CTOs, and data team leads
- Companies with fragmented data across multiple systems
- Organizations seeking to reduce manual reporting

## Key Differentiators

- End-to-end: from data collection through visualization, not just one slice
- Technology-agnostic: works with existing stacks, no vendor lock-in
- Both low-code and full-code approaches depending on client needs
- Focus on sustainable, maintainable solutions over one-off builds

## Landing Page Requirements

- Hero section with tagline and clear CTA ("Schedule a consultation")
- Four service pillars (Collect, Automate, Integrate, Display) with icons
- Social proof / trust indicators
- "How we work" process flow
- Contact form or booking widget
- Mobile-responsive, fast-loading, accessible
- SEO: target "data integration consulting", "business intelligence services"

## Competitive Context

Competitors include Deloitte (too expensive for SMBs), Fivetran (integration-only), Power BI consultancies (display-only). DIS differentiates by covering the full pipeline.

## Content Guidelines

- Avoid jargon unless immediately defined
- Lead with business outcomes, not technology
- Use concrete metrics where possible ("reduced reporting time by 60%")
- Every section should answer "why should I care?"
